Our Mission

We believe in the power of youth to drive positive change. Through education, protection programs, and humanitarian assistance, we're building stronger, more resilient communities across Sudan. Our work focuses on empowering the next generation with the skills, resources, and opportunities they need to create a peaceful and prosperous future.

Who We Are

Youth for Peace and Capacity Building is a nonprofit Sudanese organization committed to making a real difference in the lives of communities across Sudan. We work on the ground, delivering essential services and creating sustainable programs that address the most pressing needs of displaced families and vulnerable populations.

From establishing computer labs in technical colleges to distributing winter clothing and blankets to displaced families, our projects are designed to provide both immediate relief and long-term capacity building. We believe in transparency, accountability, and the power of collective action.
